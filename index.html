Ray Bradbury

August 22nd, 1920 - June 5th, 2012

Timeline of Ray Bradbury's life:

  • Born August 22, 1920, in Waukegan, Illinois.
  • Ray began writing stories when he was 11 years old.
  • Ray moved with his family to Los Angeles, CA with just $40 to their name when he was 14 years old.
  • That same year, Ray sold his first piece of writing: a joke used on the Burns and Allen radio show.
  • Ray's first published story was "Hollerbochen's Dilemma", which appeared in the magazine Imagination!.
  • Ray sold his first story at age 22, and became a full time writer at age 24.
  • His most famous books include, Farenheit 451,The Martian Chronicles,The Illustrated Man, Dandelion Wine, and Something Wicked This Way Comes.
  • Ray died on June 5, 2012 in Los Angeles.
